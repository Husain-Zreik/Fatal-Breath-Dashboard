Why Fatal Breath?

Fatal Breath is a powerful application designed to protect your family from the silent threat of toxic gases such as carbon monoxide (CO). With its intuitive features and responsive alerts, Fatal Breath acts as your home's guardian angel, ensuring you are informed and safe at all times.

The primary purpose of Fatal Breath is to significantly reduce deaths caused by high concentrations of toxic gases in homes. The app provides real-time monitoring of rooms, instantly alerting users if CO levels rise above safe thresholds.

Did you know?

Carbon monoxide poisoning causes over 400 deaths and 20,000 emergency room visits each year in the US alone.

Dashboard Overview

The Fatal Breath dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your home's air quality at a glance. Quickly see which rooms are safe and which need attention with our color-coded system.

Features:

  • Real-time CO levels for each room
  • Color-coded safety indicators
  • Quick access to room details
  • Emergency contact shortcut
  • Family status overview
Alert Notification

When dangerous CO levels are detected, Fatal Breath immediately notifies all household members with clear, actionable alerts. The app provides specific instructions based on the severity of the situation.

Features:

  • Loud audible alarm (override silent mode)
  • Visual flashing alert
  • Specific danger location
  • Recommended actions
  • One-tap emergency call
  • Quick access to family chat
Room Management

Easily manage all rooms in your home with our intuitive interface. Add new rooms, set specific alert thresholds, and view historical data for comprehensive protection.

Features:

  • Add/remove rooms with ease
  • Customizable alert thresholds
  • Historical CO level graphs
  • Device battery status monitoring
  • Room-specific notes
